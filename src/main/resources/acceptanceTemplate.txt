-----------------------------------

1) Reimbursements:
There is a limited budget for reimbursing travelling costs. This e-mail does not automatically make you eligible for reimbursements. Please see https://2017.javazone.no/speakers/monetary-policy for information about how you can apply for these.

As stated at http://2017.javazone.no/speakers speakers of lightning talks will have to provide for their own admission to JavaZone.

JourneyZone is open to all speakers and is a fantastic chance to experience the nature of Norway and make new friends! Read more and apply for a spot at JourneyZone here: http://2017.javazone.no/journeyzone. We'll start allocating JourneyZone places at the start of August.

5) Speakers dinner:
As always we'll be hosting a speakers dinner the evening before JavaZone. This year we will be eating at Paleet, just a short walk from Oslo Spektrum. You can expect more information about this in August.

For more information see: http://2017.javazone.no/speakers
